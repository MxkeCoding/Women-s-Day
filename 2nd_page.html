What is International Women’s day?


It is a day where we can celebrate and commemorate the achievements of women from all around the world. The day helps raise awareness to all people of the movement of women’s rights such as gender equality, abuse, and rights.

Strike the IWD #BreakTheBias pose



#BreakTheBias helps bring awareness to people to a gender equal world; a world free of bias; a world with accepted and valued diversities.
